# Category to IPCC Chapter Mapping

## Category 1 — Physical Climate Science Fundamentals

- WG1 Ch. 2 (Changing State of the Climate System)
- WG1 Ch. 5 (Carbon and Biogeochemical Cycles and Feedbacks)
- WG1 Ch. 7 (Earth's Energy Budget, Climate Feedbacks and Sensitivity) ← richest source

## Category 2 — Climate Data & Trend Interpretation

- WG1 Ch. 2 (observed trends — temperature, ice, sea level)
- WG1 Ch. 9 (Ocean, Cryosphere and Sea Level)
- WG1 Ch. 11 (Weather and Climate Extremes)

## Category 3 — Attribution Science

- WG1 Ch. 3 (Human Influence on the Climate System)
- WG1 Ch. 11 (Weather and Climate Extremes — attribution of specific events)

## Category 4 — Projections & Uncertainty

- WG1 Ch. 4 (Future Global Climate, SSP scenarios)
- WG1 Ch. 9 (sea level projections)
- WG1 Ch. 12 (Regional climate information, uncertainty framing)

## Category 5 — Impacts & Vulnerabilities

- WG2 Ch. 2 (Terrestrial and Freshwater Ecosystems)
- WG2 Ch. 4 (Water)
- WG2 Ch. 7 (Health and Wellbeing)
- WG2 Ch. 16 (Key Risks across Sectors and Regions) ← best synthesis chapter

## Category 6 — Policy & Governance

- WG3 Ch. 2 (Emissions Trends and Drivers)
- WG3 Ch. 3 (Mitigation Pathways Compatible with Long-Term Goals)
- WG3 Ch. 13 (National and Sub-national Policies and Institutions)
- WG3 Ch. 14 (International Cooperation — Paris Agreement, UNFCCC)
- WG3 Ch. 15 (Investment and Finance)

## Category 7 — Climate-Ecosystem Interactions

- WG2 Ch. 2 (Terrestrial and Freshwater Ecosystems) ← primary source
- WG2 Ch. 3 (Ocean and Coastal Ecosystems — acidification, marine biology)
- WG2 Ch. 5 (Food, Fibre and Other Ecosystem Products)
- WG1 Ch. 5 (Biogeochemical Cycles — carbon-vegetation feedbacks)

---

**Note:** Categories 5 and 7 both draw from WG2 but with different emphasis. Category 5 is about human vulnerability and systemic impacts; Category 7 is specifically about biological and ecological responses to climate — the distinction your background lets you draw credibly.

In total: 8 chapters from WG1, 5 from WG2, 5 from WG3.
